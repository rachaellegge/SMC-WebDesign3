UPDATE ON THE
COVID-19 PANDEMIC

Last updated April 11, 2020

Our hearts go out to those around the world who have lost loved ones as a result of the COVID-19 global pandemic, and the medical professionals on the front line of this crisis. At Inspirato, the safety and wellbeing of our members, passholders, and staff remains our highest priority. We continue to closely monitor this crisis, consulting trusted sources that include the Centers for Disease Control (CDC), World Health Organization, U.S. State Department, and our global risk monitoring partner. We are maintaining this page to share information as the situation evolves.

New Pause Feature

During this time, any Passholder in good standing who does not currently have an active reservation in place, and does not use their Pass to travel or make a booking during the months of March, April, and/or May may credit that month toward a future Pass payment as follows:

  • Your March Pass payment will be credited toward your July Pass payment.
  • Your April Pass payment will be credited toward your August Pass payment.
  • Your May Pass payment will be credited toward your September Pass payment.

Pass Cancellation Policy

As a reminder, DOVETAIL INTERNATIONAL has an extremely flexible standard cancellation policy, which we encourage you to use in your travel planning. You can cancel any Pass reservation at any time within 48 hours of your arrival date with no penalty whatsoever.
